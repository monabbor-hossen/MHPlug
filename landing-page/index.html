Simple Setup

How It Works

Up and running in under two minutes.

1

Upload & Activate

Upload mh-plug.zip via WordPress › Plugins › Add New, then click Activate. Done in under 60 seconds.

2

Open the Admin Dashboard

Navigate to MH Plug in the WP sidebar. The neumorphic dashboard gives you full control from one beautiful panel.

3

Toggle Features & Widgets

Enable only what you need. Individual toggles for every widget and module — keeping your site lightning fast.

4

Design in Elementor

All enabled widgets appear in Elementor under the MH Plug category. Drag, drop, and customize with complete visual control.
